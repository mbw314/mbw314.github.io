This experiment is a modification of a previous one. Instead of particle trajectories that evolve indefinitely, the trajectories are branches of a growing plant. The each branch has some thickness that decreases over time, and the plant has an upper limit on total "mass" that it can attain. New branches form in the same manner as the particles from the previous program.
